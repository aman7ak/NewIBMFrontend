Ocean Floor

The water pressure here is 1,086 bar. Taking a swim here is like having to balance 1,800 elephants on top of you. But even here, life has found a way to thrive. Next to sea cucumbers, white and light pink amphipods wiggle their way through the water.



And there are other things floating elegantly through the water.


Plastic bags that were found by scientists in 2018.
